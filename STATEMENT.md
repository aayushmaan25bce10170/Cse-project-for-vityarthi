PROBLEM STATEMENT
Many students and young professionals face the problem of managing monthly expenses and savings in a simple and organized way. Without overview, it is so easy to lose track of spending or not be able to save, or overspend against set budgets, resulting in financial stress.

SCOPE OF THE PROJECT
The purpose of this project is to provide a console-based budget planner that an Indian will be able to use to plan his or her monthly income against different expense categories, view calculations, and make changes in the allocations as required or deemed necessary. This is basically a no-nonsense, interactive system that needs only basic computer skills.

TARGET USERS
-Students with limited financial resources, managing daily expenses:
- Young professionals and those aiming to raise their financial awareness and improve their budgeting habits.
- Anyone who needs a simple tool for planning and monitoring monthly spending without advanced software requirements.

HIGH LEVEL FEATURES
- Input total monthly income and set percentages for common expense categories: rent, groceries, utilities, transport, others.
- Display calculated expense amounts and remaining balance. Expense percentages can be interactively edited with input validation. 
Easy, step-by-step workflow even for beginners.
